Pikachu

Pikachu is one of the most popular characters from the pokemon series. He is a short and slightly chubby yellow rodent with electrical powers. In the pokedex he is the 25th pokemon and Ash's favorite pokemon and best friend. Pikachu is the starting pokemon in the Yellow version of the Pokemon game series. Pikachu is also the evolved form of Pichu and when exposed to a thunderstone evolves into a Raichu.
